What were the instructor's strengths? Weaknesses?

  • Kristen is maybe the best professor I've had here so far. She is so approachable and friendly and helpful before, during, and after class, and she made sure to build a personal relationship with the class. I'm not normally a fan of group work, but the small group discussions we had in class were actually quite helpful. I can't think of any weaknesses. Take a class from her!

How has this course contributed to your education?

  • This class has given me a much more nuanced understanding of the history of gender and sexuality issues in the United States and around the world and different activist tactics.
  • Gender Civ 2 has been my favorite course so far. I felt I was able to really engage with the texts and discuss them on a much deeper level in class. Super fun. 10/10 would recommend
  • I learned how to look at the world differently. I recommend all students to take this course.
