SOSC 11100: Power, Identity, Resistance-1

Section 01 - Autumn 2015

Instructor(s): Hsu, Omiela
Number Enrolled: 21
Number of Responses: 13

Evaluation Comments

What were the instructor's strengths? Weaknesses?

  • Great at getting discussions going. GREAT comments on essays she hands back. Took long to give essays back but did so before the next essay prompts were given out.
  • Omie was a very engaging discussion facilitator, and maintain a good balance between having more free-form discussions and clarifying concepts from the text.
  • Tended to go off on tangents, but they were helpful tangents and added to my writing.
  • Omie was very good at directing the lectures and extremely clear. She did a really good job responding to any of our questions. Additionally, she was really helpful outside of class, providing insights into material that we may not have been able to cover during class. She was pretty genuinely invested in how we were doing. For example, she would adjust the reading schedule to ease our burden during weekdays and was generally very considerate of us. I was also a really big fan of the Chalk discussion board. I would've preferred to get papers back before 10th week and also to get the writing prompts earlier. Was also a little disappointed that we cut out chapters of Marx in order to discuss in greater depth Chapter 1. Chapter 1 is pretty important but I feel like we could've spent 1.5 classes on it at most to start and then returned to it at the very end of Marx because it probably would've been more clear. I feel like there were a lot of interesting things in Capital that we missed out on.
  • Omie was very energetic throughout every class and greatly made me want to continue taking her class. The only weakness was that it took a very long time to get our midterm papers returned. This was acceptable because of extensive comments provided.
  • Enjoyed teaching and led helpful discussions. Really enjoyed the comments on my graded paper. Sometimes, her teaching was disorganized, but nothing major and was often an attempt to adapt to what the class did not understand.
  • Spent too much time at the beginning of classes going over logistics. Otherwise an excellent teacher, very helpful commentary on essays, good at facilitating discussion, definitely knew the texts extremely well and was willing to talk to the students about them outside of class. Also good at taking the classes thoughts into account.
  • Omi is extremely enthusiastic and clearly cares about the class and the students. She made very good comments on our midterm papers, altough it did take her more than a month to grade them. Some class sessions were disorganized, with a lot of time wasted at the beginning, sometimes 30 minutes or more.
  • Omie is a very good instructor. She knows a lot about the topic and is able to facilitate very good conversations. However, sometimes discussions digress from the main topic.
  • The pedagogical style used in and out of class encouraged independent thought and a student-led environment. The instructor had a deep and nuanced understanding of the material. However, organization of class time and time management could have been far improved.
