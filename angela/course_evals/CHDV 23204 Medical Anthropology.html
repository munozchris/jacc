What, if anything, what would you change about this course and why?

  • none
  • NOTHING PLEASE
  • I would probably assign slightly less reading.
  • There should be more discussion sections and less movie clips. If we're going to have lecture slides, maybe every other class have a discussion rather that one every four classes. By the time we discussed a topic, there was no time to go over everything we'd covered, so we were constantly a week behind and had moved past the topic.
  • I would have less readings so we could focus on the most important ones.
  • More discussion sections! We barely had time to talk about everything! The discussion sections were still very useful and productive because our TA would structure them nicely, but it would have been nice to somehow have more time. Prof. Raikhel and the TA's responded to such requests during the quarter by offering additional and optional discussion sections, which I was very thankful for! However, unfortunately due to scheduling conflicts I was never able to attend any of those additional sections. So if the only way to have additional discussion sections is to make some extra ones optional, I suggest to have those scheduled ahead of time in the beginning of the quarter so that students can plan to attend in advance.
  • The option to miss one discussion question would have been very helpful. I would have preferred to just opt out of one week where I really didn't have the time to devote to a decent question instead of making my TA read some scribbles.
  • More theoretical context to the development of modern medical anthropology.
  • Probably 1 reading response every 2 weeks. Having to do one every week increases the chance that people will BS what they write just to get credit for something.
  • Some of the readings were a bit on the long side, which made the assignments for some weeks difficult to complete. Nevertheless, the readings were all useful, but they could maybe be condensed a bit for manageability.
  • Shorter chalk posts, 500 is a bit much. Maybe 300 to 500 words? Or maybe add a weekly discussion section, really enjoyed the discussion section segments of the course (which met every other week)
  • More discussion time.
  • I liked that the class was a lecture and then once in a while we had smaller discussion sections with our TA, but it made the professor seem inaccessible at times...
